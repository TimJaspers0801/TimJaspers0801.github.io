Tim J.M. Jaspers

PhD Candidate Medical Computer Vision & AI - Eindhoven University of Technology

Biography

Tim Jaspers is currently pursuing a PhD with the Video Coding and Architectures Research Group, where their research focuses on addressing the blind spots of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision applications in endoscopy. This research aims to enhance the precision and reliability of these technologies in medical settings, ensuring safer and more effective outcomes.

Contact: t.j.m.jaspers@tue.nl.
